# Items

## Obtaining items

### Rarity levels

* &#x20;Basic :small\_orange\_diamond: `Rarity of the item at the beginning of the game`&#x20;
* &#x20;Common :large\_orange\_diamond: `43,75% (1 item / 2)`&#x20;
* &#x20;Uncommon :fire: `25% (1 item / 4)`&#x20;
* &#x20;Exotic :trident: `15% (1 item / 7)`&#x20;
* &#x20;Rare :comet: `10% (1 item / 10)`&#x20;
* &#x20;Special :dizzy: `5% (1 item / 20)`&#x20;
* &#x20;Epic :star: `1% (1 item / 100)`&#x20;
* &#x20;Legendary :star2: `0,23% (1 item / 435)`&#x20;
* &#x20;Mythical :gem: `0,02% (1 item / 5 000)`

{% hint style="info" %}
You can also see them with `/rarity` !
{% endhint %}

### Item values and power

The value of an item is mainly calculated by its rarity.

The same applies to the power of the equipment. However, the power of an equipment can be limited by the power of a player. For example, a player with very little attack will not be able to take advantage of all the capabilities of a very powerful weapon. This limitation will be displayed for the player in his inventory.
